About ReWear

Sustainable fashion for everyone

ReWear was born out of a simple idea: what if we could make fashion more sustainable by making it easier for people to exchange clothes they no longer wear?

The fashion industry is one of the largest polluters in the world, with millions of tons of clothing ending up in landfills each year. At the same time, many of us have closets full of clothes we rarely or never wear.

ReWear provides a platform where you can give these clothes a second life by swapping them with others in your community. It's good for your wallet, good for your wardrobe, and good for the planet.

Our mission is to reduce textile waste by creating a circular economy for clothing. Every item swapped is one less item produced new and one less item ending up in a landfill.

We believe sustainable fashion should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford expensive eco-friendly brands. By making it easy and rewarding to swap clothes, we're democratizing sustainable fashion.

Join us in creating a more sustainable future, one swap at a time.
